Welcome to S.E.A.S!

The Social Engineering Awareness Simulator is an interactive tool designed for users with little to no IT experience to learn, practice, and stay safe from social engineering attacks. By simulating real-world scenarios like phishing, baiting, and pretexting, users can understand the risks and take proactive measures without putting their real data at risk.

How the Simulator Works

  • Step 1: Pre-Quiz – Assess your current knowledge on social engineering attacks.
  • Step 2: Interactive Simulations – Scenarios like phishing emails, suspicious USBs, or deceptive phone calls are presented in random order.
  • Step 3: Feedback – After each scenario, immediate feedback is given explaining red flags, correct actions, and lessons learned.
  • Step 4: Post-Quiz – Evaluate your learning progress after completing the simulations.
  • Step 5: Continuous Awareness – Use the Info / Notes page to review key concepts anytime.

Our Mission

To empower users to recognize and avoid social engineering threats such as phishing, baiting, and pretexting, helping them protect their personal and organizational information.

Our Vision

To create a world where users are confident and proactive in preventing social engineering attacks, reducing risk through awareness and interactive learning.
